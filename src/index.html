About Us

Bubble Tea is used to describe a quirky drink and snack in one combination. Fruit or milk teas are served ice cold or hot with chewy tapioca pearls (Boba) that you suck up through a big fat straw! Sound intriguing, tastes utterly delicious.

Boba Bros has bought Bubble Tea to Banbury. Started by two best friends, the Lock29 site is an expansion of the business which began in Leamington Spa in 2020.

Our passion is for quality drinks and desserts with Boba on them. We allow customers a freedom of choice of everything from sugar and ice levels to even having a choice of over 1000 combinations of drinks.
